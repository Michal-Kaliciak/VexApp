ATLAS Glue and Mortar Factory (now the ATLAS Group) has been established in Lodz in 1991. the founders initially operated a small center that was mainly involved in home repair, glazing and finishing only in Poland and Germany. They then got accustomed with the German products, that were far more superior in quality that the similar products available on Polish marker and decided to create their product of adhesive for the glaze.
